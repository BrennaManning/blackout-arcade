Electrical

The electrical system of the game.

Wiring

With the 32 button lights in our system, we very quickly realized the importance of strong wiring management. In our final game, a plan was made for exactly how long each wire should be to connect to the protoboards and be as neat as possible. Rails shared between buttons were daisy chained together, and the wires coming off of the buttons towards the protoboards were neatly twisted. Planning this all out in advance was extremely valuable.
